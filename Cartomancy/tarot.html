Tarot Cards: Easy Readings!

Learn how to use cards to predict others' lives!, March 5th, 2023

Tarot cards are the EASIEST way to predict divinely with cards!

Tarot cards gives people reading and insight gained through the past present and future. I recommend two different types of readings for beginnings: Three Card spread & Celtic Cross Spread. I'll briefly go over and explain these two spreads and how they work, as well as the basics of Tarot cards

What are Tarot cards?

Rider-Waite tarot cards set the standard and exactitude of hundreds of Tarot decks! I personally recommend for beginners to buy this deck, though you should ALWAYS find a deck that suits your needs and personality. For my business & personal readings I bought a Diablo deck, and a lot of people have made some very cool and intruistic designs for Tarot. Every deck is unique, and every deck can hold a meaning to you.

If you're looking for an interpersonal source to find different and unique decks, the Little Red Tarot cards is a PERFECT place to find a bunch of cool and different cards.

How do I pick the RIGHT deck for me?

Picking the right deck is ESSENTIAL for doing good Tarot readings. For beginners, I recommend going to an in-person store, or a locak book shop to see decks in person. Holding, feeling, and reading the energies of the deck help you figure out which one is made for you. HOWEVER, for all beginners I recommend THIS DECK and this would be a great starting point for basic readings!

Using your Tarot Cards!

Tarot cards can be used in multiple different manners, the two I'll be listing and discussing are: Three Card Spreads, and Celtic Cross Spreads!

Three Card Spread!

A three card reading involves these simple steps:

  • Clear your mind
  • Focus on the person you're reading(whether that's yourself or someone else)
  • Breathe deeply, and focus on the person, meld your energies, and focus.
  • Draw a card for the, "Past," "Present," "Future,"


    • Each card represents something that has happened in that category, whether it's a question, problem, occurnce, something the querent needs to focus on. Using a Tarot Meaning Guide can help you understand which card you drew.

    Celtic Cross Spread

    While Celtic Card spreads are more difficult to master, and often difficult to interpret, they are the BEST way to get exact and in-depth readings for your querent(or self)
