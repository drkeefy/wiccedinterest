Cartomancy: Getting Started!

Learn how to use cards to predict others' lives!, March 4th, 2023

Getting started with Cartomancy! Cartomancy is the art of fortunetelling, predicting, and understanding the world, it's surroundings and your deity through multiple different forms. The three main ways to predict the future with cards are: Playing Cards, using them to predict & understand the world, Tarot cards, specially-designed cards for more intristic readings, and Oracle Cards for broader and more intense readings.
