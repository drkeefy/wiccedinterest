How to protect yourself in case of emergencies in magic!

"I ain't fuckin' if she ain't got a rubber." April 22nd, 2023

Protection is important!

One of most important things involving witchcraft is protecting yourself and making sure in the case of emergencies you're okay, proteced, and not in danger at any time. Below are some of the easiest ways to help protect yourself

Grounding Rituals

The first thing I recommend when dealing with magicks or negative energies is taking a step towards Grounding. Grounding is where an individual will set themselves to the current present moment. Most of the time this can be accomplished with meditation or walking outside barefoot.

Why is this imporant? Grounding is highly essential to the human psychology. Whether you are a Witch, or a regular person, being able to take a step away from the stressors, anxiety, and paranoia of a situation. While this may seem trivial, taking a step back is highly effective.

Cleansing

One of the other major areas of protecting yourself while doing magic, is cleansing;. Cleansing removes the energy(either negative or positive), of a place, area tool, or person you're practicing on. There's a lot of research made on it, though I highly recommend incense and envisioning cleanliness. Imaging something as pearly perfect can help in this situation.

Why is this important? In the rare case that something MAJOR goes wrong, and something bad or negative enters your space, cleansing, and focusing on cleaning is a good tool to rid yourself from any negative or unappealing energies.

Caution

Needless to say one of the **BIGGEST** factors in Witchcraft is making 100% sure you know exactly what, and in some cases who, you are dealing with at the very moment in time. This would allow for you to understand and make sense of the situation if a dire situation arrises. I highly recommend doing major research and looking into things before practicing any type of witchcraft.
