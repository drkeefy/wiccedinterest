Finding your Source

Find what fuels your magic April 24th, 2023

How do you find your magic?" To put it simple, everyone witch, spellcaster, and anyone who does anything remotely close to magic is known for using something called, "Source." Source is identified in multiple different ways, though most say it requires a deity or a third party to fuel your souce. Howeverrr... it is 100% NOT required to have a deity to do magic.

So how does Source Work?

Source is the lifeline of magic, the blood, and whether it's Cartomancy, Palmistry or anything of the sort, it fuels your magic craft, it fuels just about everything. It is hardly defined simply, but I'll do my absolute best. Source is the intuition, the willpower, and focus of magic to make it y'knowww, actually work!

How do you find Source?

Source can be found in a multitude of ways. Most beginners use a deity or persona to fuel their magic. I highly recommend doing **A LOT** of research into which deity and what will work best with you. After a long trial of prayer, and though, I have come to work with two deities, Loki & Beelzebub. Two very different entites, doing research and understanding them is highly important.

Can I do magic without a deity?

I HIGHLY recommend doing magic without a deity to avoid a commmitment you're not ready for. Focus on channeling your energies and finding your Source within you. Easier said than done, but with prayer and meditation it is achievable.
